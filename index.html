Big Integer Class

Written in C++

Have you ever used C++ and been irritated by how the biggest
number you could come up with was from an unsigned long long int, which
maxes out at 18,446,744,073,709,551,616? Well then use my C++ Big class! By using
vectors I was able to rewrite basic digit-wise mathematical operators, such that you
you could easily integrate it into your own code!

Final Grade Determiner

Written in JavaFX

When finals week is approaching, somethimes you want to figure out
just how well you need to do in order to get a certain grade in a class. Well there are many
calculators that will do it for you, but I made one myself! This calculator allows you to weigh
your grades in any way, as to allow flixibility for any professor's style. The program will also
calculate exactly what you need on your final to hit the target grade!
